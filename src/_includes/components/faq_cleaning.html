Gutter Cleaning FAQs

  • In Houston, gutters should be cleaned at least twice a year — in spring after pollen season and in fall after leaves drop. Homes near large trees may need 3–4 cleanings per year to prevent clogs and water damage.

  • Clogged gutters cause water to overflow onto your siding or pool near your foundation, leading to foundation erosion, rotting fascia, mold, and costly structural repairs. Regular cleaning is far less expensive than neglect.
