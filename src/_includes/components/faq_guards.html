Gutter Guard FAQs

  • Gutter guards and gutter covers are covers or inserts that sit on top of your gutters to prevent leaves, debris, and pests from clogging them. In Houston's climate with heavy rain, large trees, and pollen, guards significantly reduce cleaning frequency and protect gutters from damage caused by debris buildup.

  • Gutter guards dramatically reduce cleaning frequency but don't completely eliminate it. Fine debris like roof grit can still accumulate. Most homes with guards only need a thorough cleaning once every 2–3 years instead of every 6 months.

  • Gutter guard installation in Houston typically costs between $500 and $2,000 for an average home depending on the type and linear footage. Micro-mesh guards cost more upfront but deliver the best long-term value. We offer free on-site estimates.

  • We recommend micro-mesh gutter guards for most Houston homes. They block fine debris while allowing large volumes of water to pass through — critical for Houston's heavy rainstorms — and outperform foam inserts and basic screen guards long-term.

  • High-quality micro-mesh guards typically last 20+ years with minimal maintenance. Lower-cost foam or plastic screen guards may need replacement every 3–7 years. We install durable aluminum and stainless steel micro-mesh guards built for Houston's climate.
